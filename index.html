Intro

ELan and nvision is annual cultural festival of IIT Hyderabad.It is one of the largest fest in south india. Starting in 2010 ,Elan is carrying its legacy since 12 years and growing exponentially year by year. It is the pride of IIT hyderabad. Apart from competitive events and illuminating talks it hosts some amazing performances by artists of international standing which mesmerise the 40,000-strong footfall from 300+ colleges. It is a 3-day long fun and frolic filled fest which is held every year in the month of February.tudents Studets hail from various institutes throughout the country and take away bagful memories and experiences.Star attractions from every possible field have continued to mesmerise the exuberant audience through their. power-packed shows and performances.
